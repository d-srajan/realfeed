## Name
RealFeed

## Short Description (132 chars max)
Spot AI-generated content instantly on LinkedIn. 100% local — no data sent anywhere.

## Full Description (up to 16,000 chars)
RealFeed adds a small AI-probability badge next to every LinkedIn post, so you can see at a glance how likely a post is to be AI-generated — before you engage with it.

**How it works**
RealFeed analyzes posts as they scroll into view, using a multi-layer detection pipeline that runs entirely inside your browser:

- **Statistical signals** — sentence burstiness, vocabulary diversity, word-length distribution, character entropy
- **Linguistic patterns** — AI phrase detection, hedging density, transition density, LinkedIn-specific structural patterns, emoji/hashtag clustering
- **Image analysis** — EXIF/metadata inspection, spectral frequency analysis for GAN and diffusion-model artifacts
- **Optional ML layer** — DistilBERT + EfficientNet-Lite ONNX models for deeper scoring (models downloaded separately)

**Privacy first**
Zero network requests. No accounts. No telemetry. Your LinkedIn feed never leaves your device.

**Smart & efficient**
Posts are only analyzed when they enter your viewport (lazy evaluation). Results are cached so each post is only scored once.

**Scores at a glance**
- 🟢 0–35% — Likely human
- 🟡 36–65% — Mixed signals
- 🔴 66–100% — Likely AI-generated

**Settings**
- Enable/disable with one click
- Three sensitivity levels: Low / Medium / High
- Toggle text and image analysis independently
