Privacy Policy

RealFeed Chrome Extension — Last updated: March 2026

Summary

RealFeed makes zero external network requests. All analysis runs locally inside your browser. No data about you, your LinkedIn feed, or your browsing activity ever leaves your device.

What data we collect

None. RealFeed does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

What the extension does locally

When you visit LinkedIn, RealFeed:

  • Reads the visible text of LinkedIn posts to run statistical and linguistic analysis
  • Reads image URLs from posts to perform frequency analysis (no images are uploaded anywhere)
  • Stores analysis results in your browser's local storage (IndexedDB) for 7 days, so posts aren't re-analyzed every time you scroll past them
  • Stores your settings (sensitivity level, enabled/disabled state) in chrome.storage.local

All of the above stays on your device and is never transmitted anywhere.

Permissions explained

  • storage — used to save your settings and the local analysis cache
  • Host permission: linkedin.com — required to inject badges into the LinkedIn page and read post content for local analysis

Third-party services

RealFeed uses no third-party services, analytics, or external APIs of any kind.

Machine learning models

If you choose to add optional ML models (DistilBERT / EfficientNet), they run entirely locally via WebAssembly (ONNX Runtime Web). No data is sent to any model hosting service.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be published at this URL and the "Last updated" date will be revised.
